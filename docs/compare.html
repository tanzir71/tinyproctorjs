What tinyproctor.js is designed for

Pick the right tool for your threat model.

Best for

  • Low-friction integrity signals for custom quiz/exam systems and LMS quizzes.
  • Heartbeat-based session tracking and configurable integrity scoring.
  • Self-hosted environments with strict privacy requirements (GDPR/FERPA).
  • Cohorts where installing software is impractical: BYOD, mobile, remote learners.

Not a replacement for

  • Secure browsers or OS-level enforcement for high-stakes certification.
  • Identity verification and recorded-evidence proctoring.
  • Proof of cheating based on a single client-side event.
